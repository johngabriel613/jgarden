Juliana's Garden

Purok Paitan Norte, Cuyapo, Nueva Ecija

Juliana's Garden is a resort and events place located in Purok 4, Paitan Norte, Cuyapo, Nueva Ecija. The resort was established in 2022 and currently employs between 10 to 20 people. The resort offers a wide range of amenities such as swimming pool, kubo and cottages, function rooms, and karaoke. Additionally, Juliana's Garden provides free wifi, various entertainment and leisure facilities. The resort also has its own food services, which include an Unli Samgyupsal dining restaurant and a Milk Tea shop.
